Lund problemsets

For some of the courses which I took at Lund University, I found it useful to create and solve a problemset of about 20 to 30 relevant problems before each written exam.

I never kept a record of where the problems are actually from. I believe they're all either made by me, or taken from the course textbooks or from Paul Dawkins's Online Notes.

If you'd like access to the problemsets and solutions, be sure to contact me and I'll be happy to share my documents.

The courses for which I have problemsets are:

  • MATA22 Linear Algebra 1,
  • MATB21 Analysis in Several Variables 1,
  • MATB22 Linear Algebra 2,
  • MATB23 Analysis in Several Variables 2.
